Relationships and dating history

News about Emma DiGiovine’s affairs with the American political commentator, Jesse Watters started doing the rounds in 2017 after Watters’ wife Noelle Inguagiato Watters filed for divorce. Noelle and Jesse met for the first time at Fox News where Noelle was Jesse’s junior.

Despite the transfer, Emma and Jesse continued dating each other. In early 2019, Jesse and Noelle’s divorce was finalized. 

Not too long after the divorce, Jesse Watters proposed to Emma DiGiovine. The political commentator shared the happy news on his Twitter account.
