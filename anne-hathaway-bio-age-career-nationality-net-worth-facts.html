Anne Hathaway - Bio, Age, Career, Nationality, Net Worth, Facts

July 2024 · 5 minute read

Anne Hathaway is an American actress. Anne Hathaway gained widespread popularity for her roles in The Princess Diaries (2001) and its 2004 sequel as a young girl, opposite Julie Andrews and Heather Matarazzo.

Early Life

Anne Hathaway was born on November 12, 1982, in Brooklyn, New York City, New York, USA as Anne Jacqueline Hathaway. She is 40 years old. Moreover, her parents are Kate McCauley Hathaway and Gerald T. Hathaway. By profession, her father is a labor attorney, and her mother is a former actress. Her father has Irish, French, English, and German ancestry where as her mother is of Irish descent.

Furthermore, her maternal grandfather was WIP (AM) Philadelphia radio personality, Joe McCauley. This actress was named after Shakespeare’s wife. She is the second child of her parents and has two brothers. Their names are Michael and Thomas.

Education

This actress joined Brooklyn Heights Montessori School and Wyoming Elementary School located in Millburn. However, she graduated from Millburn High School. There, she played soccer and took part in many plays, including Once Upon a Mattress, in which she portrayed Winnifred.

In 1993, she studied at the American Academy of Dramatic Arts and there, she became the first teenager admitted into the Barrow Group Theater Company’s acting program. Moreover, before transferring to New York University’s Gallatin School of Individualized Study, she spent several semesters studying as an English major and political science minor at Vassar College in Poughkeepsie.

Career

During high school, Anne performed in many plays. As a teenager, she was cast in the TV series titled Get Real. Also, she made her breakthrough by playing the lead role in the Disney comedy known as The Princess Diaries (2001). Moreover, she also played a recovering addict in the 2008 drama Rachel Getting Married.

After starring in a string of family films like Ella Enchanted, this beautiful actress made a transition to adult roles with the 2005 drama Brokeback Mountain. In the comedy-drama The Devil Wears Prada, she played an assistant to a fashion magazine editor.

However, it turned out to be her biggest commercial success to that point. This drama earned her a nomination for the Academy Award for Best Actress. This amazing personality went on to star in several commercially successful films such as the comedy Get Smart, Valentine’s Day, the romances Bride Wars, and Love & Other Drugs, and the fantasy film titled Alice in Wonderland.

More about the career

Following The Princess Diaries 2: Royal Engagement, she appeared in films including Havoc, and Brokeback Mountain. In addition, she starred as Catwoman in her highest-grossing film, The Dark Knight Rises, and played Fantine in the musical Les Misérables in 2012. However, this earned her the Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress.

She has since played a scientist in the science fiction film Interstellar, a haughty actress in the heist film Ocean’s 8, the owner of an online fashion site in the comedy The Intern, a con artist in the comedy The Hustle, and Rebekah Neumann in the miniseries WeCrashed.

This actress has won a Primetime Emmy Award for her voice role in the sitcom The Simpsons, sung for soundtracks, appeared on stage, and hosted events. She is a board member of an organization named Lollipop Theatre Network. This organization brings films to children in hospitals, and advocates for gender equality as a UN Women goodwill ambassador.

Hathaway is one of the most popular actresses in Hollywood of all time. The recipient of several accolades, including an Academy Award, a Golden Globe Award, and a Primetime Emmy Award, she was among the world’s highest-paid actresses in the year 2015.

Relationship Status

Anne married an actor and businessman named Adam Shulman on 29 September 2012. Fortunately, they are blessed with two sons and they are Jonathan Rosebanks Shulman and Jack Shulman. However, she has not mentioned her past affairs before Adam and always kept them behind the limelight.
