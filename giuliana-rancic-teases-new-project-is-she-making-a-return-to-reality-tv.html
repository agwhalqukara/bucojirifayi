Giuliana Rancic Teases New Project Is She Making a Return to Reality TV?

October 2024 · 2 minute read

What has Giuliana Rancic been up to lately? The beautiful television personality just posted a mysterious photo on Instagram hinting at an upcoming project. Fans of the star will remember that in 2015 she announced she was leaving her position as co-anchor on E! News — a position she’d held for nearly a decade — in order to pursue other opportunities. Although she continued to appear on Fashion Police, we’ve all been wondering what she’s been doing with all her free time.

The celebrity journalist has her husband, Bill, and her son, Duke, to keep her hands full but it looks like she’s also been working on some under-the-radar projects. Could it be a new show? Another restaurant? Or possibly the star is venturing into a new field she’s yet to try? Watch the video below to see what Giuliana shared with fans!

Obviously, fans were excited to find out that Giuliana is making a return to the spotlight. The comment section on her latest Instagram is full of predictions for what’s to come! “Hopefully it’s a daily scheduled TV show. I miss watching you every day…” one fan wrote. While another added, “I wish you’d bring back Giuliana & Bill, I miss your show so much!”

It’s clear that the 43-year-old has a strong fan base. And while the chances that she’s bringing back her old reality show (which went off the air in 2014) is unlikely, she could be working on a new reality show. Honestly, we’re just happy that Giuliana is healthy enough to pursue new ventures after her battle with breast cancer.

“I was diagnosed at 36 years old. No family history of breast cancer. It was the most shocking moment of my life,” she previously revealed to Closer Weekly in an exclusive interview. “I remember the words ‘this too shall pass.’ And it does pass. Just knowing that there is a light at the end of the tunnel is what we need to get us through the day.” We’re so happy she found the light at the end of the tunnel — and we can’t wait to see what it holds!
