She Met Joe Montanna at a Schick Razor Commercial Shoot

In the 1980s, Jennifer Montana (then Jennifer Wallace) was contacted for a Schick razor commercial, and Joe was contacted for the same commercial. Aside from being excited that she would be working with someone taller than her (a not-so-common experience for her because of her tall height), Jennifer had a strictly professional mindset when she started working with Joe.

At the time of their meeting, Joe was already an established footballer, playing in the National Football League as a quarterback. However, the model knew nothing of this information, not until they started working together.

With time, they grew fonder of each other’s company, and the football star asked Jennifer out on a dinner date. When he got the check, Joe wrote on it, asking Jennifer what her reaction would be if he asked her to spend the rest of her life with him. This was just six months into their relationship, and Jennifer was not sure she wanted to get married at that time.

She neither gave him a “Yes” nor a “No”. Since he did not get a no, Joe believed he still had a chance, and of course, he did. The player gave Jennifer Montana some time and shot again. Still on a dinner date, but he had a plane to carry the message this time. The actress said “Yes,” and the rest is history.

Jennifer Became Joe Montana’s Third Wife In 1985

In 1985, the couple got married and have stayed married since then. For Jennifer, this was her first marriage, and she did not have any legal partner before Joe. On the other hand, it was Joe’s third marriage. Before his marriage to the actress, the player had been married to two women in the past. His first marriage was to Kim Moses in 1974. He was only 18 years old at the time, and the marriage ended three years later in 1977. In 1981, he got married to Cass Castillo. His second marriage also lasted for three years. He and Castillo went their separate ways in 1984.

A year later, he got married to Jennifer, his third and current wife. The couple has been together for about 38 years now.

Jennifer Montana and Her Husband Are Blessed With Four Kids

Joe did not have any children with his first two wives. Hence, all his children were birthed by Jennifer. Likewise, Jennifer didn’t have any other child outside her marriage. The couple has four kids; two girls and two boys.

She started the business in 2013, and she donates 80% of her proceeds to The Four Rings Foundation. She started the foundation with her husband to assist families and children in the Bay Area.
