How Old is Miku Martineau?

Miku Martineau is 19 years old at the moment. She was born Miku Patricia Martineau under the zodiac sign Virgo on 14th September 2004 in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. She was born into a Christian family and was raised by her parents alongside her brother Kai. The name of her father is Peter Martineau and her mother’s name is Kumiko Martineau.

Miku is a Canadian national and has a mixed ethnicity; she is of Japanese-Canadian ancestry. While her father is Caucasian, her mother is Japanese, and was born and raised in Japan but later moved to Canada where she met Peter Martineau. For her education, Miku attended Randolph Southern Jr-Sr. High School, graduating in July 2022.

She Started Her Acting Career at Age 16

Miku Martineau started acting when she was a teenager, in 2020. Her first role came in the animated TV series Carl’s Car Wash where she voiced a character named Lina. After that and in the same year, she got another role as a voice artist in the animated series Finny the Shark where she voiced the characters Sandy/Chloe.

These were the two roles she got in 2020 and the experiences she gained from them helped her shape her skills and aspire to do more in the industry. Miku eventually rose to prominence in 2021 after she landed a lead role as Ani in the Netflix movie, Kate. This was in the actual sense her debut movie appearance and it gave her the opportunity to share the screen with notable actors like Mary Elizabeth, Woody Harrelson, and Mari Yamamoto.

In the movie which was directed by Cedric Nicolas-Troyan, Miku is the niece of a high-profile Japanese crime boss, and having a Japanese ancestry herself, she was able to perfectly interpret her role. The film introduced her to the limelight, exposing her to other producers who subsequently sought after her and gave her other roles.
