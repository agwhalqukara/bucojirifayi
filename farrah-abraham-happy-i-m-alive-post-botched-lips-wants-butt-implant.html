Farrah Abraham &quot;Happy I'm Alive&quot; Post-Botched Lips, Wants Butt Implant

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Has Farrah Abraham learned her lesson? Don’t count on it. On Monday, Jan. 26, the Teen Mom star appeared on The Doctors to talk about her recent botched lip surgery scare while putting in lip injections. And though the reality star, 23, said the experience was “a wake up call,” she admitted she’s still thinking about getting her butt done. 

“I’m still doing research,” she said of future procedures. “I don’t want anything that’s causing problems.” 

Abraham has cause for concern. On Jan. 6, she tweeted pictures of her lip injections gone wrong, writing, “Girlfriends don’t say I didn’t warn ya! #BOTCHED California #ER #fixit.” See more photos of the botched procedure.
